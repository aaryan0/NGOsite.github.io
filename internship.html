The All India Human Rights Association (AIHRA) provides internship programme on human rights for students & citizens all over India and abroad . The objectives, eligibility, requirements and other details of the internship programme are given below:

        Objective:
        
        The objective of the programme is to provide the intern an experience of working on human rights for its promotion and protection.
        
       Eligibility:
        
        The eligibility for this programme is that the applicant must be +2 pass.
        
        Expenses:
        
        All the expenses for this internship are to be borne by the intern themselves.
        
        Requirements:
        
        Applicants should submit the following documents:
        
        Curriculum Vitae.
        Two passport size color photograph.
        A scanned copy of valid and not expired ID proof.
        A cover letter detailing your expectations, objectives, and interest in the Internship Program
        The documents listed above should be sent as scanned documents either in PDF/JPG or as WORD attachments.
        
        Application Deadline
        
        All the applicants must apply with the documents to the email addresses given below.
        
        NOTE: The internship will be of 3-4 weeks period and during this period, it is mandatory for all the intern to stay in the hostel provided by the organization. After the completion of internship, each of the intern will be awarded a certificate from AIHRA.
